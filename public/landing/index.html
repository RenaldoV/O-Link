 Review, Accept, Decline

The add will be visible for a month to the pool of talent looking for jobs, who will then apply to fill your vacancy. You will be able to review the O-Link CV’s and provide provisional acceptance to those you like, and decline the others, knowing that your inbox will not be flooded as you can control the number of applications you will accept.

FAQ's

  • Who is eligible to use O-Link?

    All business looking to hire young, enthusiastic, part time staff.

     

    All individuals that have been registered at a secondary or tertiary education institution. (The app is in a beta phase and jobs are only available in Cape Town. Our expansion plans are laid out and we will be broadening our reach to other provinces soon).

  • How do I know if I have been accepted for a job?

    Under your “Current Applications” tab, a yellow dot signals a pending application, a green dot signals provisional acceptance and once the talent has confirmed they are willing to work, a blue dot will signal this confirmed acceptance.

  • What does it cost for Recruiters?

    For the first 3 months of operation, the service will be FREE for recruiters! A pricing structure will be incorporated and shared to all recruiters before the free 6 months come to an end.

  • What does it cost for Talent?

    Talent can apply for 1 job each day or they can pay:

     

    - R29 to get 3 further applications for one week

    - R49 to get 7 further applications for one week

    - R99 for a month of unlimited applications

     

     

  • I forgot my password?

    On the login screen, simply click the “Forgot Password” button and an email will be sent to you with information allowing you to reset your password.
